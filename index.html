Welcome To TICC

TICC offers free professional mental health services to students, faculty and, the staff at TIET, with a promise of confidentiality.


We aim to be the helping hand for students as they adjust to this new university lifestyle, cope with stress, and aid them in taking full advantage of TIET’s academic and social opportunities.


At TICC, students develop strategies to manage personal problems and handle situational crises. They develop self-awareness, resilience, and skills for lifelong learning.


We are here to address the multitude of mental health problems like depression, anxiety, and stress currently faced by the students.


TICC counsellors provide regular individual and group counselling sessions, workshops, and training to students, faculty, and staff on various topics from stress management, building self-worth, eating concerns, how to help someone in distress, and so forth.


We are ethically committed to confidentiality. Our mission is to serve as an anchor, ranging from personal problems to academic concerns, we at Thapar Institute Counselling Cell are always here to listen to you.



HOW IT WORKS
BOOK MY SESSION
Take the first step- book your session today! Contact the team, or counsellors, directly, to find a time slot that suits you, fill a form, and there you have your first session booked.
COFFEE WITH COUNSELLOR
Trust us, it is as interesting as it sounds. The counsellors are there to listen to you talk about anything and everything and provide you with the best possible solutions in the most amicable way. You are in the safest environment, where you can express yourself freely.
YOUR HOMEWORK
All you have to do is trust the process. Following the guidelines given by your counsellor and having regular sessions ensures a speedy transition into a better version of yourself. Our team is by your side at every step, you’ve got this!
Absolutely Confidential

Confidentiality is our esteemed code of ethics. Rest assured, any conversation with the counsellor will stay just there- with the counsellor. Unless life-threatening tendencies come forth, we maintain absolute confidentiality. We respect your privacy.
Professional Counsellors

Trained counsellors who can advise on when to go for medications. All sessions follow clinically approved methods, so you get the best possible assistance. There are absolutely no cutting corners.
Personalized Sessions

All sessions are crafted to suit individualistic needs and are customized depending on the person. The counsellors do their best to hear out all your concerns and provide solutions. ‘Listening’ is a key part of the process, and all your problems and thoughts are heard, and changes made as and when needed.
